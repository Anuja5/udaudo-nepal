Vision and Mission

If every person is included the world is more connected.

Our mission is to build the capacity of every individual in order to achieve their fullest potential and thereby bring positive change in the society where they live.

Our core businesses are: raising generation of socially responsible youth through transformative education; providing platform for youth engagement through social impact projects.

Who we are?

Udaudo Nepal is a development oriented Non-government organization working to uplift the society by providing opportunities that helps improve social conditions.It runs a diverse range of programs that aim towards improving the lives of disadvantaged communities.

We engage the youth by providing them creative workspaces to contribute for the greater good of the society.The focus areas are in the field of youth empowerment, promotion of civic engagement, gender equality, and human rights in Nepal.

Udaudo Nepal was formally registered on January 31, 2018 under the Ministry of Home Affairs, District administration office, Kathmandu Nepal. It is affiliated with the Social Welfare Council since May 9, 2018 with affiliation number 47803.
